Privacy Policy

At Jobs AI, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website.

1. Introduction

Jobs AI is an AI-powered job listing platform that leverages both advanced AI and a dedicated team to curate publicly available job opportunities. We aggregate data from open-source search engines and manual uploads, ensuring that all job listings are legally sourced and publicly accessible.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We value your privacy and, as such, do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information from our users. Our website is designed solely to display job listings without tracking your usage or gathering your personal data.

3. Use of Public Data

All job listings on our platform are sourced from publicly available data. Our team and AI work together to provide up-to-date job opportunities by linking directly to employers' career pages. We do not alter or store any of this information beyond what is necessary for display.
